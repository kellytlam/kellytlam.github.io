About

The first time I heard about R Studio was in college where I took a statistics course. I got a chance to create plots and graphs based on data from the course. I really enjoyed it a lot but at that time didn't know about the different careers that could relate back to it.

After graduating with a biology degree from UCLA, I landed a job in real estate where I worked with various real estate data such as client databases and marketing data. Starting as an administrative assistant, my role expanded over time, allowing me to gain experience in different fields. My most recent experience was with Whitestone Home Collection, a startup real estate company, where I learned how to rebrand a company, create marketing materials, and organize data to create a data studio dashboard. I enjoy teaching myself new skills using online resources and continuously strive to improve myself.
